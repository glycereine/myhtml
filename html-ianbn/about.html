ABOUT IGNITING A NATION

Our Core Values

Igniting a Nation, Inc. is 501c3 Non-Profit Ministry devoted to teaching, equipping, and connecting the dots of scripture for those who are hungry to deepen their relationship with God.

About the Ministry About Rabbi Eric

Our Committment:

Bringing the scriptures to life through weekly classes, special events, books, Israel trips, and our new TV Show: Revealing the Truth.

Our Calling:

To Ignite the Nations for the Lord and bring a unique perspective to the Living Word of God as seen through the Jewish eyes of our Executive Director, Rev/Rabbi Eric Walker.
